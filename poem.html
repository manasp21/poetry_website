Whispers of the Wind

By Elara Sands

A gentle breeze through ancient trees, Carrying tales untold, on whispered ease. It rustles leaves in verdant seas, Secrets of the earth, it starts to seize.

Sunlight dapples, shadows play, As nature's breath begins to sway. The forest listens, come what may, To stories carried from far away.

Each sigh a note, a soft refrain, Washing over hill and plain. A timeless song, again, again, The wind's soft whisper, easing pain.
